Step 3: Non-Conforming Material
When incoming material is found non-conforming:
1. Tag the material as “REJECTED” and move to the quarantine area.
2. Initiate an NCR per SOP-QA-012.
3. Notify the purchasing department and the supplier.
4. Coordinate material return or disposition with the supplier.
5. If the supplier provides replacement material, re-inspect per Step 2.
Step 4: Supplier Performance Monitoring
QA evaluates supplier performance monthly using the Supplier Scorecard:
Category Weight Metrics
Quality 40% PPM defective, NCR rate, audit score
Delivery 30% On-time delivery percentage
Cost 20% Price competitiveness, cost reduction
Service 10% Responsiveness, technical support
Scorecard results are shared with suppliers quarterly.
Step 5: Supplier Corrective Action
When a supplier’s scorecard falls below 70% or a critical NCR occurs:
1. Issue a Supplier Corrective Action Request (SCAR).
2. Requirethesuppliertoperformrootcauseanalysisandimplementcorrec-
tive action.
3. Verify the corrective action through documentation or on-site audit.
4. Place the supplier on probation if corrective action is ineffective.
Step 6: Supplier Removal
A supplier is removed from the ASL when:
• Three SCARs in 12 months without improvement
• Quality score below 50% for two consecutive quarters
• Critical safety or regulatory non-conformance
• Failure to implement corrective action within 60 days
