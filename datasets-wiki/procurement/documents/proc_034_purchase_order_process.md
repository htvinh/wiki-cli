7. Goods Receipt and Invoice Processing
7.1 Goods Receipt
When goods arrive at the warehouse or receiving dock, the Warehouse team
shall: 1. Verify the delivery against the PO 2. Inspect for visible damage 3.
Record goods receipt in the system 4. Route goods for quality inspection if
required 5. Generate Goods Receipt Note (GRN)
7.2 Three-Way Matching
Invoice processing follows the Three-Way Match principle: - Purchase Order
matches Goods Receipt matches Invoice - Anydiscrepancy shall be resolved
before payment is approved - Discrepancies include price differences, quantity
differences, and missing receipts
7.3 Payment Processing
Approved invoices with successful three-way match shall be processed for pay-
ment according to agreed payment terms. Early payment discounts shall be
taken whenever financially beneficial.
8. PO Changes and Cancellation
8.1 Amendments
Changes to an issued PO shall be made via a PO Change Order. The change
order shall be approved according to the same authority levels as the original
PO.
8.2 Cancellation
A PO may be cancelled by the Buyer with Procurement Manager approval if
the supplier has not commenced work. Cancellation after work has commenced
shall be negotiated with the supplier and documented.
