## Body
Production Line Pressure Monitoring
Purpose
This work instruction defines the procedure for monitoring and recording pres-
sureparametersonproductionlinestoensureprocesscontrolandproductqual-
ity.
Scope
This instruction applies to all production lines at Acme Corporation that use
pneumatic or hydraulic pressure in their manufacturing processes.
Equipment Required
• Calibrated pressure gauge (range: 0-10 bar, accuracy: ±0.1 bar)
• Digital pressure recorder or data logger
• Personal protective equipment: safety glasses, steel-toe boots
Procedure
Step 1: Pre-Shift Check
Before starting production each shift:
1. Verifythepressuregaugehasavalidcalibrationsticker(calibratedwithin
last 12 months).
2. Checkthatthegaugeisproperlyconnectedandshowszerowithnosystem
pressure.
3. Record the gauge serial number, calibration date, and zero reading in the
shift log.
4. If the gauge is out of calibration, notify the QA Supervisor immediately
and do not use the line.
Step 2: Normal Operation Monitoring
During production:
1. Monitor the pressure gauge continuously during operation.
2. The acceptable pressure range is specified in the product-specific setup
sheet (typical range: 4.5-5.5 bar).
3. Record pressure readings every 30 minutes in the pressure monitoring log.
4. Record the following for each reading:
• Time
• Pressure value (bar)
• Initials of the operator
• Any observations about process behavior
