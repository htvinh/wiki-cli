5.1 Renewal
The Contract Manager shall initiate renewal review 120 days before contract
expiry. The renewal assessment shall include: - Supplier performance history -
Market benchmark pricing - Alternative supplier options - Updated terms and
conditions
2

5.2 Termination
Termination may occur for convenience, cause, or expiration. Termination for
cause requires: - Written notice of breach - Cure period as specified in contract
- Final termination notice if breach not cured
5.3 Close-Out
Upon contract expiration or termination, the following actions shall be com-
pleted: -Finalpaymentandreconciliation-Returnofconfidentialinformation-
Transitionplanifapplicable-Lessonslearneddocumentation-Contractarchiv-
ing
6. Non-Disclosure Agreements
6.1 When to Use an NDA
An NDA shall be used when confidential information will be shared during the
procurement process. The standard NDA template shall be used. Deviations
require Legal Counsel approval.
6.2 NDA Management
NDAsshallbetrackedintheContractRepositorywithexpirationdates. NDAs
shall be limited to three years duration unless specific circumstances require
longer.
