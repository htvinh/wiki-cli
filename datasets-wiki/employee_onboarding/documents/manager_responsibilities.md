First 30 Days
□ Conduct first 30-day check-in (informal performance review).
□ Review progress against 30-60-90 day plan.
□ Solicit feedback from the new hire about their experience.
□ Ensure all HR paperwork and benefits enrollment is complete.
First 60 Days
□ Conduct 60-day check-in.
□ Review accomplishments and areas for growth.
□ Adjust 90-day plan as needed.
□ Encourage the new hire to provide process improvement suggestions.
First 90 Days
□ Conduct formal 90-day performance review.
□ Discuss long-term career goals and development plan.
□ Confirm the new hire has completed all onboarding requirements.
□ Transition from onboarding to regular performance management cycle.
Ongoing Support
• Continue weekly 1:1 meetings.
• Provide regular feedback (both positive and constructive).
• Encourage participation in team events and company activities.
• Support professional development goals.
