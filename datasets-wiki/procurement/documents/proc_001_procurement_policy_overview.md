1. Purpose
This policy establishes the principles and framework for all procurement activ-
ities at Acme Corporation. It ensures that all purchases of goods and services
are conducted in a transparent, ethical, and cost-effective manner while miti-
gating supply chain risk and maintaining compliance with applicable laws and
regulations.
2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and temporary staff who initi-
ate, approve, or process procurement transactions on behalf of Acme Corpora-
tion. It covers all direct and indirect spend categories across all business units
