Laptop Request Process
Overview
This document details the step-by-step process for requesting a laptop at Acme
Corp, whether for a new hire, replacement, or additional device.
For New Hires
Thelaptoprequestfornewemployeesisinitiatedbythehiringmanager. Follow
these steps:
Step 1: Submit Request
1. Navigate to the IT portal: it.acmecorp.com
2. Click “Onboarding” → “New Equipment Request”
3. Complete the following fields:
• Employee name and email
• Start date
• Job title and department
• Reporting manager
• Laptop preference: Dell Latitude 5540 or MacBook Pro (M4)
• Additional accessories needed
4. Attach the signed offer letter (HR will provide)
5. Click “Submit”
Step 2: Manager Approval
• The request is routed to the hiring manager’s manager for approval.
• Approval is typically granted within 24 hours.
• If rejected, the request is returned with comments.
Step 3: IT Processing
• IT processes approved requests within 2 business days.
• The laptop is imaged with standard software and security tools.
• Equipment is tested before release.
Step 4: Pickup or Shipping
• On-site employees pick up equipment from the IT desk (Building A, 2nd
Floor).
• Remote employees receive equipment via FedEx overnight.
• Tracking information is sent to the employee’s personal email.
