4.2 Maternity Benefit
Female employees receive: - 6 months paid leave at 100% average salary - Ad-
ditional leave for multiple births (1 extra month per child) - Prenatal checkup
leave: 5 days per pregnancy - One-time birth allowance
4.3 Retirement Pension
Employees qualify for a monthly pension at: - Age 62 (men) or age 60 (women)
with at least 20 years of contributions - Reduced pension available with 15+
years of contributions
4.4 Survivorship Benefit
In the event of an employee’s death, surviving family members receive: - Fu-
neral allowance: 10 months’ base salary - Monthly survivor pension for eligible
dependents - One-time death benefit
