1. Leave Policy
Annual Leave (Vacation)
• All full-time employees receive 15 days of paid annual leave per year.
• Leave is accrued at 1.25 days per month.
• Unused leave may carry over up to 5 days to the following year.
• Leave requests must be approved by your manager at least 2 weeks in
advance.
Sick Leave
• 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
• Sickleavemaybeusedforpersonalillness, medicalappointments, orfam-
ily care.
• For absences of 3 or more consecutive days, a doctor’s note is required.
Personal Leave
• Up to 3 days of personal leave per year for urgent personal matters.
• Personal leave is deducted from annual leave balance.
Public Holidays
Acme Corp observes 12 paid holidays per year:
• New Year’s Day (January 1)
• Martin Luther King Jr. Day (January 20)
• Presidents’ Day (February 17)
• Memorial Day (Last Monday in May)
• Independence Day (July 4)
• Labor Day (First Monday in September)
• Veterans Day (November 11)
• Thanksgiving Day (Fourth Thursday in November)
• Black Friday (Day after Thanksgiving)
• Christmas Eve (December 24)
