IT Onboarding Standard Operating Procedure
Purpose
This SOP defines the process for provisioning IT equipment and access for new
employees at Acme Corp. The goal is to ensure every new hire has the tools
they need on their first day.
Scope
Applies to all new full-time employees, contractors, and interns.
Roles and Responsibilities
Role Responsibility
Hiring Manager Submits IT onboarding request at
least 5 business days before start date
HR Notifies IT of new hire and provides
employee details
IT Support Provisions equipment, creates
accounts, configures access
New Employee Completes onboarding checklist,
acknowledges policies
Procedure
Step 1: Submit IT Request
ThehiringmanagersubmitsanITonboardingrequestthroughtheITportalat
it.acmecorp.com/onboarding. Required information:
• Employee name and email
• Start date
• Department and manager
• Equipment needed (laptop, monitor, accessories)
• Software and access requirements
Step 2: Equipment Provisioning
IT provisions the following standard equipment:
• Laptop: Dell Latitude 5540 or MacBook Pro (M4) — specified by the
hiring manager based on role requirements
• Monitor: Dell 27” 4K monitor (one standard, two upon request with
justification)
• Accessories: Keyboard, mouse, headset, webcam, docking station

Laptop Request Process
To request a laptop for a new hire:
1. Log in to the IT portal at it.acmecorp.com
2. Navigate to “Onboarding” → “New Equipment Request”
3. Complete the form with employee details and specifications
4. Submit for manager approval
5. IT processes the request within 2 business days
6. Equipment is ready for pickup or shipping by the day before start date
Forurgentrequests(lessthan5businessdays),contactITdirectlyatextension
4200.
Troubleshooting
Issue Resolution
Laptop not ready Contact IT Support — extension 4200
Account not created Check with HR that employee record is complete
Software missing Submit software request via IT portal
VPN not working Follow VPN Setup Guide or contact IT
