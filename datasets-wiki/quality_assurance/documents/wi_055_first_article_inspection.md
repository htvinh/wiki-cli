First Article Inspection Procedure
Purpose
This work instruction defines the procedure for performing First Article Inspec-
tion (FAI) on new or modified products at Acme Corporation.
Scope
Thisinstructionappliestoallfirstarticleinspectionsrequiredfornewproducts,
design changes, process changes, and supplier changes.
Definitions
First Article: The first production part produced under new or modified condi-
tions.
FAIReport: Acomprehensivedocumentrecordingtheresultsofthefirstarticle
inspection.
Dimensional Report: A record of all measured dimensions compared to the
specification.
When FAI is Required
FAI is required in the following situations:
1. New product introduction
2. Design change affecting form, fit, or function
3. Process change (new tooling, equipment, or method)
4. Material change
5. Supplier change for a critical component
6. Production relocation to a different line or facility
7. After a production shutdown of more than 12 months
Procedure
Step 1: Preparation
1. Obtain the product specification, engineering drawing, and bill of materi-
als.
2. Review the critical characteristics and special requirements.
3. Prepare the inspection plan including:
• All dimensions and tolerances from the drawing
• Material and finish requirements
• Functional test requirements
• Visual acceptance criteria per WI-042
4. Verify all measurement equipment is calibrated per WI-019.
5. Obtain the first production part or sample.
