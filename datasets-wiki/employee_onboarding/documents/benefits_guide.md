Benefits Guide
Overview
Acme Corp offers a comprehensive benefits package designed to support the
health, financial security, and well-being of our employees and their families.
Health Insurance
Plans Available
Monthly Monthly
Premium Premium
Plan (Employee) (Family) Deductible
PPO Plus $85 $320 $500
PPO Standard $55 $210 $1,000
HSA Eligible $35 $130 $2,500
HDHP
Coverage
• Medical: Doctor visits, hospital stays, prescriptions, preventive care,
mental health services.
• Dental: Cleanings, fillings, crowns, orthodontics (up to $2,000/year).
• Vision: Eye exams, glasses, contact lenses (up to $500/year).
Enrollment
• New employees enroll within 30 days of hire.
• Open enrollment is November 1–15 each year.
• Life events (marriage, birth, divorce) qualify for special enrollment.
401(k) Retirement Plan
Contribution
• Employee contributions: 1%–50% of salary (pre-tax or Roth).
• Company match: 100% of the first 3%, 50% of the next 2% (up to 4%
total).
• Vesting: Immediate for employee contributions; 3-year cliff for company
match.
Eligibility
• All employees age 21+ are eligible after 90 days of employment.
